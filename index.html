Quick Help

  • Create the event in the left panel and Lock it.
  • Add up to 50 players. Use Seed by Rating or Shuffle.
  • On Pairings tab, pick the round and click Generate Pairings.
  • Enter results with the dropdown (1-0, ½-½, 0-1, or bye).
  • Finalize the round to update standings & tie-breaks.
  • Export/Save any time; you can re-load your JSON later.

Note: Pairing engine is a tournament‑tested pragmatic Swiss that avoids repeats, tries to balance colors, and respects score groups. For elite FIDE‑level quirks, you can manually edit pairings.
